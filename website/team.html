The brains behind the structural drone

Daniel Copley

Daniel Copley is an Electrical Engineer with an emphasis in Computer Engineering. His favorite programming language is Python; however, he is fluent in several languages including: C, C++, Python, Java, Matlab, and Javascript. Professionally and academically, he has done extensive work with both Xilinx and Altera FPGA's in VHDL and SystemVerilog. Daniel has also professionally designed Printable Circuit Boards (PCBs) using Altium Designer. In his free time, he enjoys the occasional round of golf or chess, and spending time with his friends.
